About me

ID card

Hello everyone, this is Giacomo Spedicato, known in the field as Shutter Speedi. I am a London-based photographer born and raised in Manerbio, a small town in the region of Lombardy, Italy. Since my early teenage years I have developed a strong passion for photography and, ever since, I have traveled around the world to improve my skills and capture beautiful, yet ordinary moments.

Photo camera

I am specialised in portrait photography using natural light and I can operate different types of cameras. However, my best shots have always been taken with a 35 mm single-lens reflex camera, which adds that early 90s retro style to every photo.

I invite you to visit the gallery to let my pictures speak for themselves.

Graduation hat

I graduated in 2019 from the Royal Institute of Photography of London and received my licence as Certified Professional Photographer (CPP) by the Association of European Photographers (EAP) in 2020.
When it comes to editing, I am proficient using Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop, Corel PaintShop Pro and DxO PhotoLab 4.
